# Design Principles

- Identification systems must prioritize repeatability across users over expert-only accuracy
- All determinations must be traceable to reference material and method pathway
- Ambiguity must be explicitly represented, not suppressed
- Systems must handle synonymy, hybridization, and taxonomic instability by design
- Multi-method validation should be orthogonal, not redundant
- Workflows must scale from expert execution to structured non-expert operation
